Important: Ensure You Receive Our Communications

To ensure our follow-up emails reach your inbox:

1
Check your inbox for a confirmation email (which should arrive in the next few minutes)
2
Add contact@sendthesignal.earth to your contacts or safe senders list
3
If you don't see our email, please check your spam/junk folder and mark it as "Not Spam"

Select your email provider for specific instructions:

Gmail Instructions:
  1. Open the email from contact@sendthesignal.earth
  2. Click the three dots (more options) at the top right of the email
  3. Select "Add to Contacts"
  4. If the email was in spam, also click "Report not spam"
Outlook / Microsoft 365 Instructions:
  1. Right-click on the email from contact@sendthesignal.earth
  2. Select "Junk" > "Never Block Sender"
  3. You can also add the email to your contacts by right-clicking and selecting "Add to Outlook contacts"
Apple Mail Instructions:
  1. Open the email from contact@sendthesignal.earth
  2. Click the sender's name/email at the top
  3. Click "Add to Contacts"
  4. If the email was in junk, move it to your inbox
Yahoo Mail Instructions:
  1. Open the email from contact@sendthesignal.earth
  2. Click the three dots (more options)
  3. Select "Add contact"
  4. If the email was in spam, also click "Not Spam"
General Instructions:
  1. Look for options to "Add to Contacts," "Add to Address Book," or similar
  2. Check for "Mark as Safe" or "Always Allow" settings
  3. If our email is in your spam/junk folder, look for an option like "Not Spam" or "Move to Inbox"
  4. Consider adding sendthesignal.earth to your domain whitelist if you have access to your organization's email settings
